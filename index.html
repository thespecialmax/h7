About Us

Barack & Szilva Restaurant

Barack & Szilva is a restaurant with nice and pleasant atmosphere in the heart of Budapest, on the edge of lively “party-district”.

We imagined a place, where our guests can enjoy and taste the delicious, carefully prepared dishes in a calm, cozy, friendly environment. “No frills, only high-quality raw materials, expertise and hospitality.” We believe this is enough.

Our chef, György Gunst has decades of experience: the menu was set according to this. Our guests can taste the traditional Hungarian cuisine and also the delicious dishes of the French, Italian and Jewish cuisine. We have also set a wine list with the finest Hungarian wines.

Our aim was to create a home-style cuisine in a rustic-modern environment. This, combined with the heart-warming play of Kálmán Varga, the dulcimer-master creates the harmony of flavors and melodies.

Our dream came true, Barack & Szilva has opened and welcomes everyone!
